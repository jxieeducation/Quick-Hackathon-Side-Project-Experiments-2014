  1. DownloadDownload the app

  • Hardware

    Each stick has a Bluetooth accelerometer that detects when you hit in midair. The accelerometer is wireless and battery powered. Simply pair the sticks to the iPad.

  • Software

    The iPad receives data from the sticks and plays the appropriate sound sample. The iPad also detects bass drum stomps via the microphone and emulates the bass drum.

  • Useful

    Traditional drumsets are loud and expensive and electric drumsets take up space. You can use Airdrums with headphones and also with little physical space.

  • Cost Efficient

    No more buying new drum heads or accesories. Drum sounds are easily customizable so you can have multiple kits. Airdrum's hardware is also inexpensive.
